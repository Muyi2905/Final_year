Course Description:

The Introduction to Internet of Things (IoT) course provides an in-depth understanding of the fundamental concepts, principles, and technologies behind the IoT revolution. This course is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to harness the power of connected devices, sensors, and data for building smart and innovative solutions

Course Contents

  • Introduction to data structures
  • Arrays and linked lists
  • Stacks and queues
  • Trees and graphs
  • Sorting algorithms
  • Searching algorithms
  • Recursion
  • Big O Notation
  • Complexity Analysis
  • Data Structure Selection

By the end of this course, students will have a solid foundation in data structures and algorithms, enabling them to design and implement efficient solutions for a wide range of computational problems. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced programmer, this course will enhance your problem-solving skills and equip you with the tools needed to excel in software development and computer science domains.
